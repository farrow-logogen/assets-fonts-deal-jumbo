Thank you for downloading Liquide! 

LIQUIDE is created as a free typeface especially for you, created by Philip with love and a lot of coffee. You can use it for your website, for a fancy poster, or maybe even brand yourself with it. It takes a lot of work to create a font, every single glyph is made from scratch but based on existing fonts.
